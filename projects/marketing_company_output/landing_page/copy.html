Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a credit card to start?
No. Start your 14-day free trial with just your email. No credit card required, no strings attached.
How long does setup take?
Most teams are up and running in under 2 minutes. Import from Monday, Asana, or Jira takes about 15 minutes with our migration tool.
Can I import data from my current tool?
Yes. We support direct imports from Monday.com, Asana, Jira, Trello, and ClickUp. You can also import via CSV for any other tool.
What happens after my trial ends?
You can choose a paid plan or continue on our free tier (up to 5 users, 3 projects). Your data is never deleted — even if you cancel.
Is there a money-back guarantee?
Yes. We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. If TaskFlow isn't right for your team, we'll refund you — no questions asked.
Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or education?
Absolutely. We offer 50% off for verified nonprofits and educational institutions. Contact us at hello@taskflow.io with proof of status.
