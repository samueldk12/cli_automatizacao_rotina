## Overall Production Notes

- **Rendering:** 1920x1080 at 60fps, ProRes 4444 for clean alpha channel if needed
- **Color Space:** Rec. 709, sRGB delivery. HDR version optional for streaming.
- **Typography:** Inter (all text) — matches brand guidelines
- **Motion Philosophy:** Snappy physics-based animations on entrances, smooth continuous motion for ambient/holding states, spring easing for interactive feeling
- **Pacing:** Fast build (5s intro) → dense information delivery (10s product) → clear rest and CTA (5s close)
