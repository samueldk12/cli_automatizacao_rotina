## Piece 1: "First Light on the Terminator"

**Medium:** Digital painting, landscape orientation (16:9)
**Dimensions:** 3840 x 2160

### Mood Description
Awe-inspiring and vast. The first painting in the series establishes the world of NEBULA-7 — a planet caught between eternal day and eternal night. The viewer stands on the terminator line (the boundary between light and dark) looking east toward the perpetual dawn. The feeling is one of solitude, scale, and the sublime beauty of alien landscapes. This is a moment of arrival.

### Color Palette
| Color | Hex | Role |
|-------|-----|------|
| Dawn glow | `#FF8B5C` | Horizon light, atmospheric scatter |
| Deep sky blue | `#3B82F6` | Upper atmosphere transition |
| Obsidian | `#111827` | Dark side landscape silhouette |
| Warm amber | `#F59E0B` | Sun-primed mineral reflections |
| Pale gold | `#FDE68A` | Cloud illumination |
| Steel blue | `#93C5FD` | Distant mountain atmosphere |

### Key Elements
- **Foreground:** Crystalline terrain — jagged, glass-like rock formations that refract the dawn light into prismatic spectra. The crystals rise 2-3 meters from the ground, varying in height.
- **Midground:** A sweeping plain of dark volcanic soil leading to a massive cliff face. On the cliff, the silhouette of an ancient alien structure — a geometric platform with triangular pillars, partially eroded.
- **Background:** Two suns on the horizon — the primary (larger, warm amber) and the secondary (smaller, pale blue-white), their light blending into a gradient sky. Wispy atmospheric clouds catching the double sunset glow.
- **Scale element:** A lone figure (human explorer) standing on a crystal outcropping, tiny against the landscape. Suit visor reflects the dual sunrise.

### Composition Notes
- Rule of thirds: horizon on lower third, explorer at left vertical third intersection, alien ruins at right vertical third
- Leading lines: crystal formations create diagonal lines drawing eye toward the horizon and the twin suns
- Atmospheric perspective: foreground crystals are sharp and saturated, midground cliff is desaturated, background is hazy blue

## Piece 2: "The Bio-Luminescent Sea"

**Medium:** Digital painting, portrait orientation (3:4)
**Dimensions:** 2700 x 3600

### Mood Description
Ethereal, alien, and deeply quiet. The dark side of NEBULA-7 is not dead — it teems with bioluminescent life. This piece captures a vast underground ocean illuminated entirely by living light. The mood is reverent and mysterious, suggesting an ecosystem entirely independent of sunlight.

### Color Palette
| Color | Hex | Role |
|-------|-----|------|
| Bioluminescent teal | `#34D399` | Primary life-form glow |
| Deep cyan | `#059669` | Water depth, shadow areas |
| Electric violet | `#7A95FF` | Secondary glow species |
| Near black | `#0A0A14` | Absolute dark, cave ceiling |
| Pale cyan | `#A7F3D0` | Surface reflection, highlights |
| Warm coral | `#FFB18A` | Rare warm-glow organism (focal accent) |

### Key Elements
- **Ceiling:** The cavern ceiling is covered in crystalline stalactites, each tipped with violet bioluminescent organisms that hang like chandeliers
- **Water surface:** A calm, mirror-still underground sea. The teal bioluminescence creates complex rippling patterns — concentric circles emanating from unseen creatures below the surface
- **Flora:** Giant tube-like organisms (3-4m tall) line the shore, pulsing rhythmically with teal light, similar to deep-sea tube worms but massive
- **Fauna:** Translucent jellyfish-like creatures float just below the water surface, visible as ghostly shapes with internal bioluminescent organs
- **Scale element:** A small research raft with two scientists in protective suits, their headlamps providing the only unnatural light in the scene

### Composition Notes
- Vertical composition emphasizes the depth and scale of the cavern
- Ceiling-to-water-to-ceiling vertical symmetry creates a cathedral-like feeling
- The raft is placed at the golden ratio point (lower-right intersection) with the warm coral glow organism diagonally opposite
- Reflections are perfect and undisturbed except for the concentric ripples

## Piece 3: "The Floating City of Aethel"

**Medium:** Digital painting, ultra-wide panoramic (21:9)
**Dimensions:** 5120 x 2160

### Mood Description
Majestic, lived-in, and technologically advanced. The first and only permanent settlement on NEBULA-7 is a city suspended in the atmosphere by magnetic levitation. Built by a coalition of Earth nations, Aethel hovers 2km above the terminator line, never touching ground. The mood is one of human ingenuity triumphing over an hostile environment — a beacon of civilization in an alien world.

### Color Palette
| Color | Hex | Role |
|-------|-----|------|
| Titanium white | `#F3F4F6` | Primary architecture, panels |
| NovaTech blue | `#4F6FFF` | Energy conduits, glow accents |
| Storm grey | `#6B7280` | Structural elements, weathering |
| Amber sun | `#F59E0B` | Dual-sun illumination |
| Sky violet | `#A2B6ED` | Sky color, atmospheric haze |
| Carbon black | `#1F2937` | Shadow areas, mechanical details |

### Key Elements
- **Architecture:** Modular hexagonal platforms connected by tubular walkways. Each hexagon is a self-contained module — residential, agricultural, industrial, or scientific. Surfaces are white composite panels with blue energy-glow strips along edges.
- **Central spire:** The tallest structure — a 500m communications and observation tower with a glowing beacon at its peak. The spire is covered in antenna arrays and solar collection surfaces.
- **Transportation:** Small shuttle craft flying between platforms, magnetic tram lines connecting major modules. A cargo elevator descending toward the planetary surface (visible as a thin cable).
- **Background:** The dual-sun terminator line below — the warm glow of dawn meeting the dark expanse of nightside. Cloud layers between viewer altitude and ground.
- **Details:** Greenhouse domes with visible vegetation, landing pads, rotating radar dishes, solar panel arrays, a small flag of the Earth Coalition visible on one platform

### Composition Notes
- Ultra-wide panoramic emphasizes the sprawl of the city
- City spans the width, with the central spire anchoring the center-left third
- Eye is drawn left to right: residential modules (warm lit windows) → central spire → industrial zone → agricultural domes (green glow) → observation deck at right edge
- Foreground includes a close-up of a walkway railing and flag to establish viewer position within the city
- Below: atmospheric clouds and the terminator line create a dramatic diagonal horizon

## Piece 4: "The Glass Storm"

**Medium:** Digital painting, square (1:1)
**Dimensions:** 2400 x 2400

### Mood Description
Terrifying, beautiful, and overwhelming. A hyper-cane — a storm of crystallized silica particles — sweeps across the light-side desert of NEBULA-7. Wind speeds exceed 800 km/h, and the crystal particles in the storm act like billions of tiny mirrors, creating a maelstrom of reflected light. The mood is apocalyptic and sublime — nature at its most destructive and mesmerizing.

### Color Palette
| Color | Hex | Role |
|-------|-----|------|
| Blinding white | `#FFFFFF` | Core reflection, crystal highlights |
| Molten orange | `#FF6B35` | Dust-tinted lower atmosphere |
| Deep amber | `#D97706` | Mid-level storm cloud density |
| Steel blue | `#9CA3AF` | Crystal shard body color |
| Dark slate | `#374151` | Storm core, deepest shadow |
| Electric blue | `#60A5FA` | Lightning within storm (silica static discharge) |

### Key Elements
- **The storm wall:** A vertical wall of crystalline dust 10+ kilometers tall, filling the upper two-thirds of the frame. The wall is not uniform — it has tendrils, vortexes, and density variations. Light refracts through billions of crystal shards creating rainbow prismatic effects within the storm.
- **Lightning:** Blue-violet electrical discharges within the storm cloud — silica dust creates massive static charges. Branching lightning visible in multiple locations within the wall.
- **Foreground:** A research outpost built with reinforced triangular structures, anchored deep into the bedrock. The outpost's shields are active — visible as a faint blue hexagonal energy field that is being impacted by crystal particles (small impact flashes visible).
- **Scale element:** The outpost is tiny (barely visible) and the storm fills the frame, showing the immense scale of the weather event.
- **Sky:** Above the storm wall, the sky transitions from the storm's orange-tinted underbelly to clear blue, then to the amber of the perpetual sun.

### Composition Notes
- Square format creates a "wall" effect — the storm fills the frame from bottom to near-top
- Strong vertical composition: ground → outpost → storm wall → clear sky
- Outpost placed at bottom-center, providing anchor and scale
- Lightning branches create leading lines within the storm
- Color transition from warm (bottom) through cool (mid) to warm (top) creates visual tension

## Piece 5: "What Lies Beneath"

**Medium:** Digital painting, landscape (21:9)
**Dimensions:** 4800 x 2057

### Mood Description
Mysterious, ancient, and revelatory. The final piece in the series. Deep beneath the surface of NEBULA-7, in a cavern system untouched for billions of years, the colonists have discovered evidence of prior intelligent life. Not ruins — a library. A vast underground chamber filled with information-storage crystals, each containing encoded data in an unknown format. The mood is one of profound discovery mixed with the weight of cosmic time.

### Color Palette
| Color | Hex | Role |
|-------|-----|------|
| Crystal white | `#F9FAFB` | Data crystal primary glow |
| Deep space blue | `#131E5E` | Background void |
| Information gold | `#FCD34D` | Active data crystal glow |
| Ancient teal | `#6EE7B7` | Secondary crystal species |
| Violet memory | `#A2B6ED` | Archive section markers |
| Warm human | `#FDE68A` | Human exploration lights |

### Key Elements
- **The Library:** A cavern so vast its ceiling is lost in darkness. The "walls" are actually millions of crystal columns arranged in a grid pattern, extending into the darkness. Each column is 10-15m tall, translucent, with internal light patterns that suggest stored data.
- **Active crystals:** A few crystals near the foreground are illuminated brighter than others (gold glow), suggesting they are currently "active" or responding to human presence
- **Exploration area:** A small research platform with equipment — holographic displays showing decoded patterns (abstract geometric shapes, mathematical sequences), scanning equipment pointed at nearby crystals
- **Human figures:** Five scientists in varying poses — one reaching out to touch a crystal, one studying a holographic display, one taking measurements, two observing in awe
- **Details:** Faint energy "currents" visible flowing between crystals like optical fiber — a dim glowing network. Floor markings (alien geometric patterns) that glow faintly in response to the crystals' activation
- **Background:** The crystal grid extends into infinite darkness, with occasional flashes of different colored crystals (teal, violet, gold) hinting at the vastness and variety of the archive

### Composition Notes
- Ultra-wide panoramic emphasizes the scale and depth of the cavern
- Strong perspective lines: the crystal grid recedes to a vanishing point in the center background
- Foreground (platform + scientists) occupies lower quarter of frame
- Lighting: the primary light sources are the crystals themselves — no artificial lighting visible, the scene is illuminated by the data glow
- Color grading shifts from warm gold in the foreground (active crystals nearest humans) to cooler teal and violet in the middle ground (dormant sections) to deep blue-black in the background (unexplored depths)
