1 Executive Summary

Based on comprehensive market analysis of the Steam tower defense genre, MYC Game Studio recommends developing "Siege of the Crystal Keep", a 2D pixel-art tower defense game with a unique dynamic-map mechanic. The project has a favorable risk-reward profile with an estimated 4.3x ROI in the moderate scenario.

The decision was reached through a multi-department collaborative process using the MYC Game Studio plugin system, which activated 9 specialized departments to analyze, design, and prototype the complete project.

  • Art Style: Pixel art with modern lighting (proven by Kingdom Rush success)

  • Engine: Godot 4.x for production, Python/Pygame for prototyping
  • Platform: PC via Steam with planned DLC strategy
