# Especialista: Growth Hacker
**ID:** `growth_hacker`
**Department:** Vendas / Inovacao
**Arquivo:** `plugins/specialists/growth_hacker.py`

## Descricao

Especialista em growth hacking — Metricas AARRR, loops virais, programas de referral, experimentos de crescimento, estrategia de conteudo SEO, product-led growth.

## Arquitetura

- **Tipo de Hook:** PRE_LAUNCH, CONTEXT
- **Variavel de Ambiente:** `MYC_PLUGIN_GROWTH_HACKER=active`

### Contexto Injetado

- **Framework AARRR (Pirate Metrics):** Acquisition, Activation, Retention, Referral, Revenue — metricas detalhadas para cada fase
- **Loops Virais:** Inherent, Embedded, Stimulated, Word of Mouth — calculo de K-factor, tempo de ciclo
- **Growth Experiment:** Hipoteses, priorizacao ICE/PIE, design de experimento, analise com rigor estatistico, 20-40 experimentos/trimestre
- **SEO Content Strategy:** Topic clusters, keyword research long-tail, content gap analysis, backlink building, SEO tecnico
- **Product-Led Growth (PLG):** Freemium/free trial, onboarding self-service, viral loops, expansion via uso, PQLs (Product Qualified Leads)
